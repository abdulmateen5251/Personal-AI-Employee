# 📋 Weekly CEO Briefing Schedule

> [!info] Schedule Details
> - **Frequency:** Weekly
> - **Day:** Monday
> - **Time:** `07:00` (before business hours)
> - **Status:** ✅ Active

## Briefing Contents

Each Monday briefing auto-generates and includes:

1. **Executive Summary** — system health snapshot
2. **Revenue** — MTD tracking vs goal
3. **Completed Tasks** — done items from past 7 days
4. **Bottlenecks** — items stuck in Pending_Approval > 24h
5. **Proactive Suggestions** — cost optimization, subscription flags
6. **Upcoming Focus** — priorities for the week ahead

## Workflow

```
Monday 07:00 → Auto-generate → Vault/Briefings/YYYY-MM-DD_Monday_Briefing.md
```
